<!--SR:!2023-07-15,4,287-->

<!--SR:!2023-08-03,16,287-->

<!--SR:!2023-07-12,1,247-->

$tan(A+B+C+D.....)$ :: $\frac{S_1-S_3+S-5}{S_2-S_4+S_6}$ where $S_n$ is the sum of the products of the tangents taken $n$ at a time
